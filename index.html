Fast, Private Instagram Reel Audio Extraction

InstaAudio is an in-browser audio extractor tailored for content creators, video editors, and social media managers. Powered by WebAssembly (FFmpeg.wasm), InstaAudio lets you isolate, extract, and trim clean MP3 audio tracks from Instagram Reels, TikTok videos, and MP4 clips directly inside your device's browser memory.

Why Use InstaAudio?

  • 100% Client-Side Privacy: Video files are processed locally on your phone or computer CPU. Your media clips never touch an external server or cloud storage bucket.
  • Extract Trending Sound Bytes: Grab trending background songs, dialogue, and audio clips from saved Reels to reuse in your own video edits.
  • Precision Sound Trimming: Extract exact audio segments or sound bites by specifying start and end timestamps.

How to Extract Audio from Instagram Videos

  1. Download or save your desired Instagram Reel / video clip to your phone or computer.
  2. Select the clip in InstaAudio using the file selector above.
  3. Choose your target format (MP3, AAC, M4A, or WAV).
  4. Optionally set start and end timestamps to isolate a specific sound byte.
  5. Click "Extract Reel Audio" to generate your instant high-quality audio download.
